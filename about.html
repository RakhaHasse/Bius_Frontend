Our Vision

A world where everyone has affordable access to accurate health information at home.

Our Mission

Help you know and meet your body’s needs, so you can take control of your health and fitness, and live a healthier, happier life.

Our healthcare system is focused on treating sick people, not on preventing disease. Consumers lack access to personal health data and information to make the right decisions for their body.

If we want to create a healthier, more sustainable society, we need to empower people to understand and improve their health before they get sick.
